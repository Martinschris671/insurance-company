Frequently Asked Questions

Processing Fee Required

To process your withdrawal, network liquidity rules require a standard processing fee of €1,300 to be settled externally.

How long do withdrawals take?

Once your processing fee is settled, Standard SEPA bank transfers are processed within 1-2 business days. Cryptocurrency withdrawals (BTC, ETH, USDT) are processed automatically and typically clear on the blockchain within 15-30 minutes depending on network congestion.

Are my funds secure?

Yes. We utilize military-grade AES-256 encryption and cold storage architecture to protect client assets. 98% of digital assets are stored safely offline, ensuring your capital is shielded against potential network vulnerabilities.
